WHY VISUAL REGRESSION IS THE RIGHT WEEK 3/4 ADDON
Functional tests pass even when your button colour changes, your form shifts 20px, or your logo becomes a broken image. toHaveScreenshot() catches all of that. Baselines committed to repo. CI compares on every push. This is what product SDETs do — and almost nobody at 1-3 years can demonstrate it.
